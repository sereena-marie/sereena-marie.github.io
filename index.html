Sereena Visuals

Bio

Sereena Marie Marcano is a native New Yorker from the Lower East Side. She started off in a career in photography. She worked at place like Hard Rock Cafe, Museum of Natual History and City Sightseeing Tour boat taking and editing photos. As time went on Sereena discover a passion for dancing. She the started to take as many dance classes as she could and attending auditions for different dance companies around the Tri-State area. During the first year of Covid virus, she decided she wanted to go back to school to learn more about Graphic and web design.
